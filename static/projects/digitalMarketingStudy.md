### Description
Working at the intersection of business intelligence and data tracking, I collaborated on a comprehensive interdisciplinary market study to analyze how global industry leaders utilize digital media ecosystems to maintain consumer loyalty. Focusing on Nike as our primary case study, my role centered on conducting a detailed competitive analysis of audience engagement across their specialized social media pipelines. I mapped out the various digital consumer touchpoints, studying how they leveraged keyword-rich captions and dynamic video arrays rather than passive hashtags to drive conversions. Based on performance trends, our team delivered data-driven optimization strategies, explicitly recommending advanced audience segmentation through the creation of hobby-specific sub-channels (such as Nike Yoga and Nike Pilates) to maximize local relevancy. This experience demonstrated how systems-thinking and data evaluation translate directly into commercial corporate strategy and consumer behavior modeling
