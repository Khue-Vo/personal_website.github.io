### Situation
As a beginner in crochet, I undertook the project creation, intended to create a small pouch with interesting design to store my cards.

### Obstacle
Because I am a beginner, crochet with pattern design is rather a challenging work. Moreover, there are no pattern with clear instruction for me to follow. So I pay extra attention to the instruction video to not miss a thing and count my stitches. However, I still crochet it wrong and have to redo 3 times. The firstime, I cannot form a sun because there was too little stitches. The second time, I cannot form a square because there are too many stitches.

### Reflection
This experience taught me the art of being calm and patient. I learned that there are nothing wrong with making mistakes because it is alright, I can always start again. Moreover, I realize that crocheting is not different than programming. Both require attention to details, logical thinking, and being patient. Now, I am calmer when facing issues during programming. Instead of stressing, I dedicated my time to troubleshooting the issue logically.
